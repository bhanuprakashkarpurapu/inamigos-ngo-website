About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization established in 2020 with a mission to create positive social impact across India. The foundation works towards education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and community development through volunteer-driven initiatives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities through education, social welfare, skill development, and sustainable initiatives while creating opportunities for a more inclusive and compassionate society.

Our Programs

  • Project SEVA - Food and clothing distribution for the needy.
  • Project BACHPANSHALA - Educational support for underprivileged children.
  • Project JEEV - Animal welfare, rescue, and feeding initiatives.
  • Project UDAAN - Women empowerment and skill development.
  • Project PRAKRITI - Environmental conservation and tree plantation drives.
  • Project VIKAS - Employability and skill development programs.

Our Impact

  • 50,000+ beneficiaries supported
  • 200+ volunteers across India
  • Presence in 28 states
  • 20,000+ trees planted through environmental initiatives
